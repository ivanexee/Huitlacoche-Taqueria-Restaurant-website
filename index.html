About

Our Story

Welcome to Huitlacoche Taqueria Restaurant, a family-owned establishment that has been serving authentic Mexican cuisine in Queens since 2015. Our journey began with a simple dream: to share the rich, vibrant flavors of traditional Mexican cooking with our community.

The name "Huitlacoche," inspired by the prized Mexican corn truffle, reflects our commitment to authentic and unique flavors that can't be found anywhere else.
